## Decision Rule

For this project, homepage primary keywords should meet all three conditions:

1. Real demand: normally `200+` monthly searches for medium/large markets, or `50+` only if the keyword is very exact and transactional.
2. Rankable: ideally KD `0-30`; KD `30-50` can be secondary; KD `50+` should not be the first homepage target for a new site.
3. SERP fit: top results must match an online tool, diagnosis, AI try-on, quiz, or personal-color intent. High-volume tutorial, ecommerce, or ambiguous terms should be pushed to supporting pages, not homepage.

## Content Architecture Warning

Some recommended homepage terms overlap with existing localized SEO pages, especially personal-color/colorimetry pages. If the homepage takes these as primary terms, the supporting pages should be reframed as long-tail pages such as:

- free online test
- how the analysis works
- seasonal palette guide
- makeup colors by undertone
- lipstick/foundation subtopics

That prevents the homepage and inner pages from competing for the exact same query.
